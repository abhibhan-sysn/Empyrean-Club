Introduction

The Empyrean Club came into existence in May 2023 and was officially registered in August 2023 under the Department of Botany, St. Xavier’s College, Ranchi (Reg. ID: SXC/EC2023/BOT).

Objectives of the Empyrean Club:

  1. Foster creativity, collaboration and innovation among members.
  2. Focus on personality enhancement and professional development of students.
  3. Conduct initiatives aimed at social welfare and assisting the underprivileged.
  4. Offer a platform for skill-building and knowledge enhancement in relevant domains.
  5. Organize, sponsor or support conferences, seminars, symposia, exhibitions and meetings, along with arranging lectures and demonstrations on topics related to Microbiology.
  6. Plan and execute activities, events and projects at both national and international levels.
  7. Create and sustain an inclusive and encouraging atmosphere for all members.

Membership Eligibility:

Students from any department who have an interest in the club’s vision and activities are welcome to join.
